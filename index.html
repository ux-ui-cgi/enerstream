Welcome to enerstream agency services inc.

EnerStream Agency Services Inc. is an integrated energy company delivering natural gas and renewable agency, consulting and procurement services to Industrial, Commercial, and Institutional customers throughout Canada. We focus on helping customers manage their natural gas costs through leading edge procurement strategies, flexible products, transparent billing and dedicated customer service. Our team of highly skilled energy professionals work closely with each client to develop an energy strategy that will help them achieve their business goals. EnerStream, along with our parent company, Rockpoint Energy Storage and our ultimate owner, Brookfield Infrastructure Partners, all support Canada’s goal of net zero greenhouse gas (GHG) emissions by 2050 or sooner. EnerStream is well positioned to play a key role in the Energy Transition for our clients with experience and access to Responsibly Sourced Gas (“RSG”) and Renewable Natural Gas (“RNG”). EnerStream is also diligently tracking developments in the areas of Hydrogen and Liquified Natural Gas (“LNG”).
